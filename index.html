Information About Me

I was born in Johannesburg, South Africa, and now live in Germany with my Swiss/German wife and our growing family. I spent 15 years as a professional cyclist, competing in high-profile races like the Tour de France and traveling to 37 different countries.

Since retiring from professional cycling at the end of 2020, I've shifted my focus to data analytics and gained valuable experience in the field. I've worked as a customer service manager, where I analyzed customer behavior data to identify trends and improve the overall customer experience. I then transitioned into a role as marketing manager, with a focus on public relations and athlete sponsorship. Despite this shift, I continued to leverage my skills in data analysis to gain deeper insights into customer behavior and inform our marketing strategy.

In my latest role, I've chosen to join the journey of Tudor Pro Cycling Team as a cycling coach, where I guide and mentor young riders, and use my data analytics skills to help optimize their performance.

As a former professional athlete, I've developed a wide range of soft skills that have served me well in my career. I learned the importance of effective communication, both on and off the bike, as well as self-motivation and adaptability. I was responsible for my own success as a cyclist, but also had to work closely with teammates and coaches to achieve our shared goals. I also developed strong networking skills, building relationships with sponsors and members of the cycling community. Finally, I learned the importance of team building, both in terms of creating a strong and cohesive team culture and working collaboratively with colleagues across departments.

I studied business and management at the University of Essex, where I gained a solid foundation in key business principles. After years of analyzing my own training data, I developed a passion for data analytics, which led me to pursue a career in this field. I completed a data analytics bootcamp through CareerFoundry, where I learned to use tools like Excel, PostgreSQL, Tableau, and Python

I'm motivated by a desire to keep expanding my skill set and exploring new opportunities. Data analytics has become a passion of mine, and I'm excited to see where this field takes me. I'm also committed to continued growth and education, which is why I recently enrolled in a BSc Data Science program through the IU Internationale Hochschule in Germany. I believe that staying curious and open-minded is essential to both personal and professional success, and I'm eager to continue learning and growing.
